Qualitative ROI Drivers
Driver TODAY — Current State WITH SHOPIFY — The Gain
Scalable Growth Without Linear Headcount The only lever is to hire more people — linear, expensive, and hard to justify. Self-serve ordering absorbs volume without adding headcount. The same team can manage more accounts at higher revenue.
The Long Tail is Currently Untapped Mom-and-pop accounts are deprioritized because reps have no capacity to onboard and manage them. That revenue is left on the table. Self-serve unlocks a new buyer segment that requires zero rep time to transact — turning overlooked accounts into a compounding revenue stream.
Every Order is a Manual Error Risk Orders arrive by email, are cross-referenced against inventory manually, and re-keyed into the ERP. Each touchpoint is a chance for a mistake, a delay, or a lost order. Orders flow directly from the portal into the ERP via API — eliminating re-keying, reducing errors, and giving customers real-time confirmation.
Competitive Risk of Inaction Chains are actively requesting online ordering. Every month without a portal is a reason for them to look elsewhere. A live B2B portal becomes a reason to win and retain chain accounts that are evaluating whether Action Sales can scale with them.
Human Touch, Amplified — Not Replaced Reps spend the bulk of their time processing repeat orders — transactional work that adds no relationship value and crowds out consultative selling. Reps are freed to focus on cross-selling, relationship building, and landing new accounts — the work that actually drives revenue and retention.
